Podrozownik is a web application for motorcyclists. Basically app gathers people who are looking for ideas and company for travels. Without an account one can search for routes, see comments and rates. Furthermore app recommends best travels based on popularity. When logged in user may create his own trip and invite his friends. If created trip is public users can send requests to join the trip.
